Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students ask before joining us.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a completely free 3-day trial with no registration fee and no credit card required. Simply contact us on WhatsApp to get started immediately.

Are teachers male and female separate?

Absolutely. We have dedicated male teachers for brothers and dedicated female teachers for sisters. You can choose your preferred teacher when enrolling.

What platform is used for online classes?

All live classes are conducted via Zoom or Skype — whichever you prefer. Both platforms are free to use and work on mobile, tablet, and computer.

What age groups do you teach?

We teach all age groups — from young children (5 years+) to adults of any age. Our teachers are trained to adapt their teaching style to every age group effectively.

How do I pay the monthly fee?

Fees are paid monthly via bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, or international payment methods. Our team will guide you through the payment process on WhatsApp.
