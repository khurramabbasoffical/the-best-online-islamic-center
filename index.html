FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students ask before joining us.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a completely free 3-day trial — no registration fee and no credit card required. Simply contact us on WhatsApp and we will set up your first class within hours.

Are there separate male and female teachers?

Yes, 100% separate. We have dedicated certified female teachers for sisters and dedicated certified male teachers for brothers. You can choose your preferred teacher at enrollment.

What software do you use for classes?

All live classes are conducted via Zoom or Skype — whichever you prefer. Both are free to download and work on any phone, tablet or computer easily.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept students from 4.5 years old all the way to adults of any age. Our teachers are specially trained to adapt their teaching style effectively for every age group.

How can I pay the monthly fee?

Fees can be paid monthly via bank transfer, PayPal, or secure online cards. For Pakistan-based families, Easypaisa and JazzCash are also accepted. Our team will guide you fully on WhatsApp.
